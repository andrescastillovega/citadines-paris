Family & baby fit

Air-conditioning for early-September nights (three of the eighteen lack it), room for a cot, kitchen and laundry access, how nap-quiet the rooms are, playgrounds or pools within reach, and stroller practicality — Paris métro stations are mostly stairs, so flat walkable areas and bus routes count.

Guest reviews

Current Booking.com, Tripadvisor, Trip.com and Expedia ratings, weighted toward 2025–26 stays and normalised to 0–5. Exact source figures are inside each card.

Location & transit

Walkability to the headline sights and charm of the immediate streets; métro lines within a short walk plus direct airport and main-line links — a big deal on arrival day with a stroller, a cot bag and luggage.

Neighborhood safety

Day and night comfort of each district plus recurring guest remarks — pickpocket zones near Châtelet and Place d'Italie, the Pigalle strip after dark, station-square grit at Gare de Lyon. All eighteen are in normal, visitable areas; this grades comfort, not danger.

Facilities status & value

Renovation date and current condition plus what's on site — A/C, kitchens, pools, laundry, lifts, gardens, parking. Three properties carry 2026 renovation flags; see their cards. Value grades what the September rate buys relative to the other seventeen.
